Guide

Step 1: Define a search term concerning one of the following properties of the projects in interest:

  • Project Title, Acronym, ID, Objective
  • Coordinator, Country of Origin
  • Call, Programme, Funding Scheme

Step 2: Select frameworks, filters and sorting parameters to refine the search further to narrow down on projects of interest.
Step 3: Explore the search results concerning projects and participating organisations.
Step 4: Select a project to get more information concerning the consortium and breakdown.
Step 5: Save projects to the bookmark panel for ease-of-access at a later stage.
